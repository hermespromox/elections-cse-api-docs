Important before using the API

The upstream backend only permits browser CORS calls from the official government website. Interactive “Try it out” execution is disabled here. Public consultation is quota- and CAPTCHA-controlled; respect 429, 503, and retryAfter. This documentation is not affiliated with or endorsed by the French Ministry of Labour.

What the public backend exposes

From establishment identity to union-level results

01

Establishment search

SIREN, SIRET, normalized company name, department filtering, sorting, and pagination.

02

Election metadata

Cycle, college, round, election month, transmission status, IDCC, and professional categories.

03

Ballot statistics

Registered voters, turnout, blanks, valid votes, quorum, lists, seats, and electoral quotient.

04

Union results

Union and confederation labels, list ballots, candidate votes, gender, and elected status—without candidate names.
